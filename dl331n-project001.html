World Wide Web (WWW) is a collection of HTML(HyperText Markup Language) documents which is the basis of most of the webpages. HTML pages are connected by Hyperlinks or URL.

HTML is made up of:
 - text content
- markup (made up of tags)
- references to other documents
- links to other pages
